TNREGINET – Tamil Nadu Registration Department

TNREGINET is the official online portal of the Registration Department, Government of Tamil Nadu. The portal provides digital access to property registration services, encumbrance certificate, guideline value search, marriage registration and document verification facilities.

What is TNREGINET?

TNREGINET helps citizens avoid long queues at Sub-Registrar Offices by offering online access to important land and registration services. It ensures transparency, security and convenience in property transactions.

Key Objectives

  • Digitization of registration records
  • Transparency in property transactions
  • Reduction of corruption and middlemen
  • Easy public access to documents
  • Faster service delivery

Services Available on TNREGINET

Encumbrance Certificate (EC): Check property transaction history.

Guideline Value Search: Know government-approved land value.

Document Search: Verify registered property documents.

Certified Copy: Apply for certified copies online.

Marriage Registration: Online marriage registration services.

Stamp Duty Details: Updated stamp duty and registration fees.

How to Apply for Encumbrance Certificate

  1. Visit TNREGINET official portal
  2. Login using registered mobile number
  3. Select Encumbrance Certificate option
  4. Enter property details
  5. Submit application
  6. Download EC after approval

Importance for Property Buyers

  • Verify ownership authenticity
  • Avoid disputed properties
  • Check loan and mortgage details
  • Ensure legal and safe registration

User Registration Process

  1. Visit TNREGINET portal
  2. Click user registration
  3. Enter mobile number and email
  4. Verify OTP
  5. Create login credentials

Benefits of TNREGINET

  • 24×7 online access
  • Time and cost saving
  • Secure government system
  • Transparent information
  • User-friendly interface

Common Issues

  • Old document data not available
  • OTP delay
  • Server busy during peak time

Conclusion

TNREGINET has simplified property registration in Tamil Nadu by providing digital services, transparency and ease of access. It is an essential platform for buyers, sellers and landowners.
